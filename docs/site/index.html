How Does Fetch Work?

Fetch gives you real time visibility into your business by organizing and prioritizing data from your lead generation marketing efforts. In addition to assisting you in your marketing efforts, Fetch My Guest allows you to gain a better understanding of your sales agents performance in real-time... in or out of the office!

Fetch My Guest will provide keen insights into your vacation rentals business by providing:

  • ● Real-time notification of leads/bookings weekly, daily, and monthly.
  • ● Capturing advertising sources and prospective guest information automatically on every lead.
  • ● Identifying the best performing 3rd party listing sites to maximize your marketing dollars.
  • ● Measure your efficacy of leads. Track how many of your leads turn into sales.
  • ● Provide automated follow-up tools to turn your leads into bookings.
  • ● Track how long it takes for your agents to follow up on leads
  • ● Track all off your sales agents correspondence in one place.

About Us

Fetch My Guest was born from the need to solve vexing problems around managing day to day marketing efforts in an organized, efficient, and affordable manner. The team is comprised of expert individuals with backgrounds in Vacation Rental Property Management, Hospitality Marketing, Social Marketing, and Technology Development.


The team has spent countless hours developing, refining, and testing the Fetch My Guest application as real sales agents in a vacation rental office. We use Fetch My Guest everyday, we understand the vacation rental industry, and we continue to grow our vision in delivering a world class vacation rental application that adds true value to your vacation rental business.
